About Us

The ACM Tula's Chapter is a vibrant community dedicated to advancing the field of computing through education, research, and practical application. We provide a platform for students to connect, learn cutting-edge technologies, and work on real-world projects.

Our mission is to foster innovation and excellence in computing by organizing workshops, hackathons, guest lectures, and collaborative projects. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, there's a place for you in our chapter.

Join us to develop your skills, network with industry professionals, and contribute to impactful projects that make a difference in the tech world.
